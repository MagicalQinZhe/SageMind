SageMind 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 5 月 31 日

概览

SageMind 是一款离线优先的本机 AI 助手。我们不会要求你创建账号，也不会在应用中加入广告 SDK、分析 SDK 或第三方追踪 SDK。应用的主要设计目标是在你的设备上完成聊天和内置技能任务。

SageMind 目前不运营用于接收或分析用户聊天内容的服务器。除非你主动使用需要网络的功能，例如下载模型、导入远程个人技能、打开外部链接或使用依赖第三方网页的技能，否则你的聊天输入和技能内容会尽量留在本机处理。

我们收集的数据

我们不会主动收集、出售或共享你的个人信息。SageMind 不包含用于广告定位、跨应用追踪或用户画像的 SDK。

应用可能在你的设备本地保存以下内容：下载的模型文件、tokenizer 和配置文件；语言、主题和最近使用模型等偏好设置；你添加的个人技能目录信息；以及部分技能产生的本地数据，例如收藏的语录、收藏的单词或心情记录。

本机处理与模型下载

SageMind 会在首次加载模型时通过网络下载模型文件。下载服务提供方可能会按其自身政策接收常规网络信息，例如 IP 地址、请求时间和设备网络信息。模型下载完成后会缓存在你的设备上，后续常用功能可以离线优先运行。

你可以在应用中删除已下载的模型文件。删除应用也会移除由系统管理的应用本地数据。

个人技能与第三方服务

如果你导入 GitHub、HTTPS URL 或本地目录中的个人技能，SageMind 会读取你选择的来源。对于远程来源，请求会发送给对应的网站或服务，例如 GitHub 或你输入的 HTTPS 地址。

某些技能可能会打开外部链接、嵌入第三方网页，或调用设备能力来展示结果。例如地图类技能可能使用第三方地图网页，邮件类技能可能打开系统邮件能力。这些第三方服务会按照其自己的隐私政策处理数据。

权限

相机权限仅用于需要摄像头输入的视觉类技能，例如 Text Spinner。SageMind 不会把相机内容上传到我们自己的服务器。你可以在系统设置中关闭相机权限。

本地文件夹权限仅在你主动选择目录导入个人技能时使用。SageMind 只读取你授权的目录内容来加载技能文件。

数据控制

你可以通过应用内的模型管理功能删除已下载的模型。部分技能提供删除或清空本地记录的功能。你也可以通过卸载应用移除系统为 SageMind 保存的大部分本地数据。

儿童隐私

SageMind 并非面向 13 岁以下儿童设计。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

联系我们

如果你对本隐私政策或 SageMind 的数据处理方式有疑问，可以通过 GitHub 提交反馈： https://github.com/MagicalQinZhe/SageMind/issues

Privacy Policy

SageMind Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Overview

SageMind is an offline-first on-device AI assistant. We do not require you to create an account, and the app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs. The app is designed to complete chat and built-in skill tasks on your device.

SageMind does not operate servers that receive or analyze your chat content. Unless you actively use a feature that needs the network, such as downloading models, importing remote personal skills, opening external links, or using skills that rely on third-party web content, your chat input and skill content are processed on device whenever possible.

Data We Collect

We do not actively collect, sell, or share your personal information. SageMind does not include SDKs for advertising targeting, cross-app tracking, or user profiling.

The app may store the following data locally on your device: downloaded model files, tokenizer and configuration files, language, theme, and recently used model preferences, personal skill directory information, and local skill data such as saved quotes, saved words, or mood records.

On-Device Processing And Model Downloads

SageMind downloads model files over the network the first time you load a model. The model download provider may receive standard network information under its own policy, such as IP address, request time, and device network information. After the model is downloaded, it is cached on your device, and common workflows can run in an offline-first experience.

You can delete downloaded model files in the app. Uninstalling the app also removes app-local data managed by the operating system.

Personal Skills And Third-Party Services

If you import personal skills from GitHub, an HTTPS URL, or a local directory, SageMind reads the source you choose. For remote sources, requests are sent to the corresponding website or service, such as GitHub or the HTTPS address you enter.

Some skills may open external links, embed third-party web pages, or use device capabilities to show results. For example, map-related skills may use third-party map pages, and email-related skills may open system email capabilities. These third-party services process data under their own privacy policies.

Permissions

Camera permission is used only for visual skills that need camera input, such as Text Spinner. SageMind does not upload camera content to our own servers. You can turn off camera permission in system settings.

Local folder access is used only when you actively choose a folder to import personal skills. SageMind reads only the directory content you authorize to load skill files.

Data Control

You can delete downloaded models through the model management feature in the app. Some skills provide options to delete or clear local records. You can also uninstall the app to remove most local data stored for SageMind by the operating system.

Children's Privacy

SageMind is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or SageMind's data practices, contact us through GitHub: https://github.com/MagicalQinZhe/SageMind/issues
